Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a traditional brazilian dessert used mainly in birthday parties. It's cheap, easy to make and delicious. If you like chocolate, you'll love this dessert!

Ingredients

  • Four spoons of chocolate powder
  • One can of condensed milk
  • One spoon of butter
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Mix the chocolate, the butter and the condensed milk in a pan
  2. Put it under low heat and stir it until it stops sticking to the bottom of the pan
  3. Remove it from the pan
  4. Roll the brigadeiro into small balls and put the sprinkles around it
