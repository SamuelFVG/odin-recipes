Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

This is a fast, cheap and tasty dessert that can be made within less than 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of brown sugar

Steps

  1. Melt the butter over medium heat
  2. Stir in the brown sugar and the milk
  3. Boil the mixture for 2 minutes, stirring frequently
  4. Remove it from heat and stir in the peanut butter
  5. Let it chill in a bowl and cut it into squares when it gets firm
  6. Enjoy!
