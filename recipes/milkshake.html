Banana Milkshake

Description

This recipe is good if you're on the rush and need something delicious, fast and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 120 grams of berries
  • 750 ml of milk
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients into the blender
  2. Blend it until the mix looks homogeneus
  3. Pour it and enjoy!
